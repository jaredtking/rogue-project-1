What is Gone Phishin'?

Gone Phishin' is a quiz of 25 yes/no questions. The question is, do you trust this piece of content? The content might be an e-mail, a social media message, or a web page.

At the end of the quiz we show you how well (or not!) you did.

We only show screenshots of suspicious conent so there is no risk by taking the quiz.

Who made this?

Gone Phishin' was created by Team Rogue for a senior software project.

Team Rogue consists of Cameron Beckfield, Cameron Fowler, Jack Lestina, and Jared King.
